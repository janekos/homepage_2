Me, Myself & I

Hello! Thanks for visiting my page! My name is Janek Kossinski and I'm an unestablished developer from Estonia.

This page was supposed to be my portfolio, but during the development I figured that making it my main everything wouldn't be that bad of an idea. This way I can keep everything in one place.

On this page you'll find all kinds of thingies that are made by or concern me, including: random fun projects I feel like uploading here, maybe blog posts and funny cat pictures.

The guy on the left (or up) with a notebook that has "CHILL" written in it is me. It was taken during the "defence of my seminary paper", which is pretty much a smaller thesis in my university. I'm not that confident in how this picture looks, but usually I'm incredibly unphotogenic, so for now this should suffice for describing my general appearance.

Below you'll find what skills I own, my hobbies, my traits, things I do and don't like and everything in between, so this page is pretty much my CV.
